Want to write for PakistanTravelBlog.com? HERE’S HOW!

GUEST POSTS

Do you have a passion for travel or know more about certain destinations than most people?

Well, if that sounds like you, then you are in luck because Pakistan Travel Blog is accepting guest posts from both female and male writers! We want to add more voices, opinions, stories, and tips to this site.

We want to bring in people out there who have helpful information and insight we might not have, especially because we can’t travel everywhere on the planet.

WHAT KIND OF GUEST POSTS DO WE ALLOW?

  • Visa tips or processes to different countries from Pakistan
  • Travel stories to different countries around the world that begin from Pakistan
  • Travel itineraries and tips that you followed in any foreign country
  • Your travel stories and experiences within Pakistan (e.g. Experiencing the magical autumn in Hunza Valley)
  • Food and travel guide of any destination (e.g. Top 10 food/desert everyone must try in Lahore)
  • Travel tips from your travels (e.g. Top 10 tips for traveling in Karachi as a solo female foreign traveler)
  • Travel tips for different styles of travelers (e.g. List of clothes to pack for a trekking holiday to Swat Valley)
  • Dos and Don’ts for tourists in your village/city town (e.g. What should foreign Youtubers “not” do in Peshawar?)
  • Lists of outdoor things in Pakistan (e.g. Top 10 places to visit in Sahiwal)
  • Anything outdoor-related from across Pakistan (travel story, tips, travel guide, inspiration, resources, news, etc.)
  • The historic relevance of tourist attractions in Pakistan (e.g. List of less explored historical areas in Baluchistan)

WHAT KIND OF GUEST POSTS DO WE NOT ALLOW?

  • Commercial post that doesn’t really help people
  • Anything related to tour operators, travel agents, brands/companies, etc.

What do I get out of contributing to Pakistan Travel Blog?

You will be able to include only one of the following:

  • One backlink in the body of your post linking back to your blog/website.
  • Link to your social media profile (Instagram, Facebook page, Twitter, etc.).

Do you have any guidelines to follow?

  • The content must be unique and not have been posted anywhere else
  • Must be a minimum of 500 words
  • We would like it if you can include pictures or videos (you will be given an email address after submitting your guest post below)

Finally, please note that if you send us a draft and we find that it will be too much work to edit, we reserve the right to reject it. Additionally, there is a chance that we’ll ask you to make changes to your draft so please expect notes and rewrites. That’s really it!

INTERESTED IN SUBMITTING A GUEST POST?

Then fill out the form below including your guest post. Upon submitting, you will be provided an email address for sending images, embeds, and other details.

 

About US

Pakistan Travel Blog aims to help all travelers (international and domestic). We provide tips, resources and guides (travel, food, visa, etc).

We also accept and publish trip reports and guest posts that can actually help other fellow travelers.

We cover all destinations of Pakistan from Khunjerab in the north to Karachi in the south. Join our mailing list to stay connected to us. Read more!
